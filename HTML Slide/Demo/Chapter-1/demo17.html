Physical Searches

One way that you might prevent weapons from entering your site is by conducting searches of individuals and vehicles.

When conducting physical searches of individuals, you may be required to use an electronic wand device.

Electronic wand devices may be used when you suspect that an individual is attempting to bring a concealed weapon into your site.

Always begin an electronic wand search by asking the individual to stand with his/her arms extended and legs shoulder-width apart.

It is important to perform searches in a particular order so that you do not miss any key search points on the individual.
