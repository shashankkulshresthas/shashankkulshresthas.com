Weapons of Mass Destruction

Chemical weapons are extremely dangerous and usually have immediate effects on people.

These weapons can be inhaled, swallowed or absorbed through the skin.

They are easy to make and readily available.

Examples of chemical weapons include cyanide and sarin.

Explosive weapons are used in approximately 70% of terrorist attacks.

These types of weapons are commonly used because they are dramatic and can easily be detonated from a distance.
