Introduction

Since the tragic events of September 11, 2001, the terms ‘terrorism’ and ‘weapons of mass destruction’ have become commonplace.

It used to be the case that training on these topics was given only to law enforcement personnel. That has since changed, and it is now extremely important that security officers are properly trained and knowledgeable as well.

This course will provide you with the tools to help you become a more effective security officer.

You will learn why your worksite might be a potential terrorist target and what you can do to help prevent a possible terrorist attack.
